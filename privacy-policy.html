Privacy Policy

This privacy policy provides important information about how some of your data may be collected and processed by us when you use one of our applications.

What data do we collect and what is it used for?

All apps and games that collect data will do so for analytics, ads and tracking purposes. None of our apps or games will be linked to your identity. We will use the information we collect to provide a better game/app experience to both you and others who play our games.

With who may your data be shared and why?

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your prior consent in countries where collecting users’ consent may be required by applicable laws and regulations. When you consent to the collection of data for advertising and analytics purposes, we may share data with the following categories of recipients:

    1. Ad Partners which allow us to monetize the ad inventory of our apps and provide users with free products and services. Those partners usually collect data via their own tools (Software Development Kits or “SDKs”). Below we provide a list of our partners and their respective privacy policies. This will allow you to exercise your rights directly toward them hereafter:
      1. Ad Colony (https://www.adcolony.com/privacy-policy/)
      2. Chartboost (https://answers.chartboost.com/en-us/articles/200780269)
      3. Google AdMob (https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en)
      4. Unity Ads (https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy)
      5. Vungle (https://vungle.com/privacy/)
    2. Analytic companies which provide us with tools and technologies that allow us to better understand how users interact with our services with the overall aim of improving our service delivery practices. These companies usually collect data via their own SDKs. The policies of our partners that implement analytical SDKs are as follows:
      1. GameAnalytics (https://policies.google.com/privacy)
      2. Google Analytics (https://policies.google.com/privacy)
    3. Authorities only to the extent we are obliged by applicable laws and regulations.

Children data

We never knowingly or willingly collect any personal data concerning children under 16 years of age.

International

Some of the partners and processors referred in this policy are located outside of the European Union. In these cases, we ensure that:

  1. the personal data is transferred to countries recognised as offering an equivalent level of protection or,
  2. for personal data transferred outside of countries recognised by the European Commission as having a sufficient level of protection, any of the mechanisms offering appropriate guarantees is used, for which provision is made by applicable regulations, and notably the adoption of the standard contractual clauses of the European Commission.
